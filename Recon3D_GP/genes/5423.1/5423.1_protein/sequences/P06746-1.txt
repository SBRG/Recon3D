CC   -!- FUNCTION: Repair polymerase that plays a key role in base-excision
CC       repair. Has 5'-deoxyribose-5-phosphate lyase (dRP lyase) activity
CC       that removes the 5' sugar phosphate and also acts as a DNA
CC       polymerase that adds one nucleotide to the 3' end of the arising
CC       single-nucleotide gap. Conducts 'gap-filling' DNA synthesis in a
CC       stepwise distributive fashion rather than in a processive fashion
CC       as for other DNA polymerases. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11805079,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21362556, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9207062,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9572863}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Deoxynucleoside triphosphate + DNA(n) =
CC       diphosphate + DNA(n+1).
CC   -!- COFACTOR:

CC       Note=Binds 2 magnesium ions per subunit. {ECO:0000250};

CC   -!- PTM: Methylation by PRMT6 stimulates the polymerase activity by
CC       enhancing DNA binding and processivity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16600869}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated at Lys-41, Lys-61 and Lys-81: monoubiquitinated
CC       by HUWE1/ARF-BP1. Monoubiquitinated protein is then the target of

CC       leading to degradation by the proteasome. USP47 mediates the
CC       deubiquitination of monoubiquitinated protein, preventing
CC       polyubiquitination by STUB1/CHIP and its subsequent degradation.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19713937, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21362556}.
