CC   -!- FUNCTION: Error-prone DNA polymerase specifically involved in DNA
CC       repair. Plays an important role in translesion synthesis, where
CC       the normal high-fidelity DNA polymerases cannot proceed and DNA
CC       synthesis stalls. Favors Hoogsteen base-pairing in the active
CC       site. Inserts the correct base with high-fidelity opposite an
CC       adenosine template. Exhibits low fidelity and efficiency opposite
CC       a thymidine template, where it will preferentially insert
CC       guanosine. May play a role in hypermutation of immunogobulin
CC       genes. Forms a Schiff base with 5'-deoxyribose phosphate at abasic
CC       sites, but may not have lyase activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11013228, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11251121,
