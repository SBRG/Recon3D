CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 7,8-dihydroneopterin 3'-triphosphate = 6-
CC       pyruvoyl-5,6,7,8-tetrahydropterin + triphosphate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1282802}.
