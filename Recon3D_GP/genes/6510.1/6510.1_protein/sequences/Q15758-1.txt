CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-dependent amino acids transporter that has a
CC       broad substrate specificity, with a preference for zwitterionic
CC       amino acids. It accepts as substrates all neutral amino acids,
CC       including glutamine, asparagine, and branched-chain and aromatic
CC       amino acids, and excludes methylated, anionic, and cationic amino
CC       acids (PubMed:8702519). Through binding of the fusogenic protein
CC       syncytin-1/ERVW-1 may mediate trophoblasts syncytialization, the
CC       spontaneous fusion of their plasma membranes, an essential process
CC       in placental development (PubMed:10708449, PubMed:23492904).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10708449, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23492904,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8702519}.
